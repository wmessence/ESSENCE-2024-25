Good evening lovely ladies!

Speaking of love…HAPPY VALENTINES DAY!!!

Whether you have a valentine or not, I hope you all are enjoying your Friday; that you ate some chocolate or strawberries (or chocolate-covered strawberries) today.

We thank all of you who attended our Alumni Hours event last Monday! It was a fantastic turnout and our guest speaker Alex Yeumeni was lovely.

ESSENCE has a few announcements for you:

Applications for the 2025-26 ESSENCE Exec Board are live! We encourage you to apply and contribute to the growth and longevity of our organization. Here is the full link itself in case the embedded form malfunctions: https://forms.gle/QnfSRvXY5ZXEFHJS9

Saturday, February 15th, 2025 at the Entrepreneurship Hub – ESSENCE x BSO: Stepping Into Excellence Black Expo. There will be a variety of Black vendors selling items from crafted goods to clothing. Come on and treat yourself and support a Black-owned business!

Tuesday, February 18th, 2025 – Queer Black Liberation Talk with the CSD. Catered food and critical conversations!

Friday, February 21st, 2025 – Taste of Soul with the CSD: Sample cuisines from across the black diaspora. From 11AM-2PM. Come and enjoy some lovely, culturally rich food.

March 25th, 2025 at 7PM, Chancellor’s 113 - ESSENCE x The Women’s Network: Women’s History Month Trivia!

That’s all I have for you as of now, but I wish you all a restful weekend. Do whatever you need to do to reset and be ready for next week. Stay warm and take care of yourself. You all are valentines of ESSENCE.

Lastly, I want to again draw your attention to the fact that our member dues are $7. It is important to pay member dues so that we may be able to fund our fun social gatherings. I will keep reminding you all throughout the semester. You can pay this in cash or through cashapp to the ESSENCE account - $essencetreasury.

Thank you all for supporting ESSENCE. We can’t wait to see you again!

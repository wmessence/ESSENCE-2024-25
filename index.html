November 1, 2024


Happy, happy Friday to the lovely members of ESSENCE!

First, I’d like to thank everyone who attended our Black Professionals Social last Friday! It was a wonderful evening, where Black students had the chance to speak to Black professionals who have achieved in the business world, academia, health, and more! I hope you had a great learning experience, and that you keep any advice the professionals gave you and store it somewhere safe for the future. As this is an annual event, our organization would like to be informed on any notes our attendees might have so that we are able to improve the Black Professionals Social with each year that comes. That being said, I encourage you all to fill out this feedback form  and express your thoughts and suggestions.
On Monday, November 4th, 2024 at 6pm in Chancellor’s 114, Essence Women of Color is having another general body meeting. This meeting will be our Cookies & Coloring Night, so please come out for an entertaining night of cookies (obviously), candy, and cutesy coloring pages! It’s calm. It’s low-stress. It’s just us girls. Come on over!

Be on the lookout for our last meeting of the semester: December 2nd, 2023 at 6:30 PM - General Body Meeting → Cozy Christmas Night! (Chancellors 113). More information to come soon!

Lastly, I want to again draw your attention to the fact that our member dues are $7. It is important to pay member dues so that we may be able to fund our fun social gatherings. I will keep reminding you all throughout the semester. You can pay this in cash or through cashapp to the ESSENCE account - $essencetreasury.

Thank you all for supporting ESSENCE. We can’t wait to see you again!

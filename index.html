January 24, 2025


Welcome back, ladies, and HAPPY NEW NEW YEAR!

The wind is freezing, the paths are icy, and our whole campus is hidden under a blanket of pure white snow. Welcome to the start of spring semester at William & Mary. I hope that you all enjoyed your winter break, whether you stayed home or traveled abroad. That was a blissful month, but now we’re back and ready for round 2!

ESSENCE has a few announcements for you:

This Saturday, January 25th, 2025, at 5:00PM in Sadler Center ESSENCE Women of Color will be present at William & Mary’s Activities Fair. Come find us and ask your friends to tag along! Spread the word!

Next Thursday, January 30th, 2025, at 5:00PM in the Sadler Center’s Commonwealth Auditorium, the Center for Student Diversity has invited ESSENCE to speak at their MLK Commemoration. This event involves a fireside chat with renowned Martin Luther King Jr. scholar Dr. Clayborne Carson. A formal dinner will follow, free for all. Please take some time to attend this profound event in an effort to honor an even more profound legacy.

On Monday, February 3rd, 2025 at 6:00PM in Chancellors 113, in collaboration with the W&M Alumni House, ESSENCE Women of Color will be hosting an alumni event! We will be joined by an alum of our very own, who will take us through her journey from her time at W&M and onward. Come along and listen to her story to gain some helpful insight–and yes, there will be food.

On Tuesday, February 18th, January, 2025, in collaboration with the Center for Student Diversity and several other organizations, ESSENCE will be engaging in a Queer Black Liberation Talk. We will have an in-depth discussion on the intersectionality of queerness and women. There will be catered food present as well. Be sure to join us to explore this important topic!

Lastly, as this is the final semester in ESSENCE’s 2024-25 academic year, we are introducing our elections for the year 2025-26! The following positions are open:

  • President
  • Vice-President
  • Secretary
  • Treasurer
  • Events Coordinator
  • Public Relations Manager

Please demonstrate your interest in being a part of the ESSENCE Executive Board here. Don’t worry, the election process is very lowkey. Indicate what positions you are interested in using the form, and during our March monthly meeting, we will give all applicants a chance to talk about their motivations for being on Exec. No pressure, pure fun and passion!

That is all I have for you all so far, but more is definitely to come. I hope you all had a calm first day of classes, and shoutout to that lovely, lovely snow day that gave everyone an extra day off. The semester hasn’t yet picked up, but set up your calendar and your daily routines. Keep your motivations and mantras, spend time with friends, and call home. Happy first week back and have a wonderful semester!

Lastly, I want to again draw your attention to the fact that our member dues are $7. It is important to pay member dues so that we may be able to fund our fun social gatherings. I will keep reminding you all throughout the semester. You can pay this in cash or through cashapp to the ESSENCE account - $essencetreasury.

Thank you all for supporting ESSENCE. We can’t wait to see you again!

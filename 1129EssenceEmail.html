November 29, 2024


Hey everyone!

If you’re reading this email, you know what that means–it's Friday! Unfortunately us making it to the end of the week means our Thanksgiving break is also wrapping up 🙁. I hope you all are enjoying your time at home with your loved ones, and that you had a lovely Thanksgiving yesterday! I just know the food was good…and to think we have to go back to Sadler.

ESSENCE has a little gift for you when you get back from break:

This Monday, December 2nd, 2024 at 6pm in Chancellor’s 114, Essence Women of Color is having another general body meeting. However, this is no ordinary meeting. As it is our last meeting of the semester, we intend to go out with a bang! We will be hosting a Holiday Celebration to close us out for the year. There will be activities such as ornament making and designing holiday cards. Following the theme, we’ll have hot chocolate and marshmallows, as well as some lovely cupcakes. Of course, there will be a classic movie playing in the background. So pull up in your holiday (or regular) PJs and enjoy a fun evening with us!

RSVP through our Tribelink here. Please RSVP. It’s very important for the visibility of our organization at W&M so that we are able to sustain ESSENCE and do more. Pretty please!

This Thursday, December 5th, 2024 at 5:30PM, ESSENCE Women of Color is collaborating with the Center for Student Diversity and several other Black organizations on campus to host the annual Pre-Kwanzaa Celebration. This is a wonderful way to end our fall semester, as we review the core principles of Kwanzaa as a community. This event is free and open to all but please be sure to RSVP before attending. See you there!

Lastly, I want to again draw your attention to the fact that our member dues are $7. It is important to pay member dues so that we may be able to fund our fun social gatherings. I will keep reminding you all throughout the semester. You can pay this in cash or through cashapp to the ESSENCE account - $essencetreasury.

Thank you all for supporting ESSENCE. We can’t wait to see you again!

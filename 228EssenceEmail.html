ESSENCE has a few announcements for you:

Monday, March 3rd, 2025 at 6PM, Chancellor’s 113, ESSENCE will be hosting elections for our 2025-26 Exec Board! If you applied, be sure to prepare to explain why you want the role. Nothing crazy; we encourage you to hype yourself up! We implore everyone to join us to vote on who will be leading ESSENCE for the following year.

There is still time to apply!

Applications for the 2025-26 ESSENCE Exec Board are still live! They close Sunday at 5PM. We encourage you to apply and contribute to the growth and longevity of our organization. Here is the full link itself in case the embedded form malfunctions: https://forms.gle/QnfSRvXY5ZXEFHJS9

March 25th, 2025 at 7PM, Chancellor’s 113 - ESSENCE x The Women’s Network: Women’s History Month Trivia! Join us to interact with new students and get to know more people. I heard through the grapevine that there will be enticing prizes for the winning teams! And yes, it will be catered.

That’s all I have for you as of now, but I wish you all a restful weekend. Do whatever you need to do to reset and be ready for next week. Soak up the warm weather, see your friends, and set aside time for yourself!

I will keep reminding you all throughout the semester. You can pay this in cash or through cashapp to the ESSENCE account - $essencetreasury.

Thank you all for supporting ESSENCE. We can’t wait to see you again!

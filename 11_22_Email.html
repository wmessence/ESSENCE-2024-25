WE MADE IT!!!

Oop–I forgot to say hi. Hi! Happy Friday everyone! This is no regular Friday, however. We finally made it to Thanksgiving Break! What a wonderful feeling. I hope your week went well (or as minimally chaotic as possible). If it didn’t, at least it's over and you have one whole week to rest and decompress (essentially, bedrot).

ESSENCE has a little gift for you when you get back from break:

On Monday, December 2nd, 2024 at 6pm in Chancellor’s 114, Essence Women of Color is having another general body meeting. However, this is no ordinary meeting. As it is our last meeting of the semester, we intend to go out with a bang! We will be hosting a Holiday Celebration to close us out for the year. There will be activities such as ornament making and designing holiday cards. Following the theme, we’ll have hot chocolate and marshmallows, as well as some lovely cupcakes. Of course, there will be a classic movie playing in the background. So pull up in your holiday (or regular) PJs and enjoy a fun evening with us!

RSVP through our Tribelink here. Please RSVP. It’s very important for the visibility of our organization at W&M so that we are able to sustain ESSENCE and do more. Pretty please!

Lastly, I want to again draw your attention to the fact that our member dues are $7. It is important to pay member dues so that we may be able to fund our fun social gatherings. I will keep reminding you all throughout the semester. You can pay this in cash or through cashapp to the ESSENCE account - $essencetreasury.

Thank you all for supporting ESSENCE. We can’t wait to see you again!

November 8, 2024


Good afternoon and happy Friday everyone!

I hope your week was lovely and calm, and if it wasn’t, here’s the weekend! Make sure you take some time to rest, reflect, and decompress. ESSENCE has a few announcements for you all this week:

First, thank you to everyone that came out to our last meeting on Monday. Cookies & Coloring Night was all we were hoping it would be: calm, low-stress, and creative! I hope you enjoyed your time there, coloring and chatting and devouring those chocolate-chip cookies with us (admittedly, I had three and took two for the road). Overall, a great meeting! That brings us to our upcoming one. It seems far away but with all these exams and projects, this meeting will be a perfect window to relax a little bit.

On Monday, December 2nd, 2024 at 6pm in Chancellor’s 114, Essence Women of Color is having another general body meeting. However, this is no ordinary meeting. As it is our last meeting of the semester, we intend to go out with a bang! We will be hosting a Holiday Celebration to close us out for the year. There will be activities such as ornament making and designing holiday cards. Following the theme, we’ll have hot chocolate and marshmallows, as well as other foods. Of course, there will be a classic movie playing in the background. So pull up in your holiday (or regular) PJs and enjoy a fun evening with us!

Lastly, I want to again draw your attention to the fact that our member dues are $7. It is important to pay member dues so that we may be able to fund our fun social gatherings. I will keep reminding you all throughout the semester. You can pay this in cash or through cashapp to the ESSENCE account - $essencetreasury.

Thank you all for supporting ESSENCE. We can’t wait to see you again!
